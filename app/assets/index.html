As government forces continuously struggle to stop the unrest, armed vigilante groups emerge to restore law and order. Calling themselves the “Bulls”, they help some sectors of the market recover. Whereas other vigilantes, the “Bears” take up arms to create more chaos and accelerate the decline, shorting civilization itself and profiting on the way down.


Join a faction, enter the chaos and battle to win NFTs and acquire zones of land that produce even more in-game utility.

Technical
overview

Occupy Wall Street is a first of its kind web based NFT game launched on the Solana Smart Chain. The core of the game revolves around rewarding players with tokens for engaging in battle for land zones which are themselves valuable NFTs.

Additional characters and battle capacity can be developed to further grow a player’s ability to exert influence in the game world. Battles happen between the “Bull” and “Bear” factions as well as the “Hedge Fund” NPCs.
